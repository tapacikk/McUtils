Constructs a Cartesian coordinate from a bond length, angle, and dihedral
and three points defining an embedding

    > first coordinate defining the embedding
  - `xb`: `np.ndarray`
    > third coordinate defining the embedding
  - `xc`: `np.ndarray`
    > third coordinate defining the embedding
  - `r`: `Any`

  - `a`: `Any`

  - `d`: `Any`

  - `ref_axis`: `Any`
